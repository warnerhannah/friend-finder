# friend-finder

The Friend Finder application is a matchmaker for finding people similar to you. 

How to partake: 
* Step 1: Take the survey. Answer all 10 questions and click submit
* Step 2: See who, from our API, is the most similar to you based on the questions you answered.

The purpose: 
* To join people of similar lifestyles and interests together
